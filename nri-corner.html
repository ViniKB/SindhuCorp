Why Mangalore

Coastal Karnataka's most liveable city — and a smart investment

Mangalore combines infrastructure growth, strong rental yields, a large NRI community, and emotional connection for families with roots in Dakshina Kannada.

✈️ Excellent connectivity

International airport, NH-66 highway, New Mangalore Port, daily direct flights to Dubai, Abu Dhabi, Muscat and more.

📈 Stable appreciation

Consistent 8–12% annual capital appreciation in prime residential corridors like Gandhinagar, Bejai, and Kadri over the past decade.

🏥 World-class amenities

Top hospitals (KMC, AJ, Yenepoya), premier schools (Delhi Public School, Canara), and India's highest literacy rates.

🏖️ Quality of life

Beaches, cooler coastal climate, clean air, low pollution, strong Konkani/Tulu/Kannada cultural roots — a genuine home for your family.

The process

Six simple steps — most of our NRI buyers never visit Mangalore during purchase

1. Initial consultation

Share your budget, configuration, timeline and preferred neighbourhoods over WhatsApp. We shortlist 2-3 options aligned to your goals.

2. Virtual walkthrough

Live video visit + 360° tour + drone footage of the neighbourhood. Ask anything — we answer honestly.

3. Booking & agreement

Token booking via NRE account. Digital sale agreement delivered to you for e-signature.

4. Loan & finance

We coordinate NRI loan sanction with our banking partners — typically 10-15 working days.

5. Registration

Via Power of Attorney executed at your local Indian consulate, or plan a 2-day trip — we handle the sub-registrar appointment.

6. Possession & handover

Full handover with family member, video-recorded inspection, snag list, keys. Post-possession, we stay in touch.

NRI FAQs

Common questions from buyers abroad

What documents do I need as an NRI buyer?

Your passport, visa/residency, PAN card, Indian address proof (if any), overseas address proof, NRE/NRO bank statement, and KYC documents. Our team shares a full checklist upfront.

Can I pay from my overseas bank account?

Payments must come through an NRE, NRO or FCNR account, per FEMA rules. We cannot accept payments directly from foreign bank accounts. We help you open an NRE account if needed.

Do I need to come to India to register the property?

Not necessarily. Most of our NRI buyers register through a Power of Attorney granted to a family member. Alternatively, a 2-day trip is enough — we coordinate the sub-registrar slot.

What about rental management after I buy?

We partner with vetted rental management firms who handle tenant sourcing, rent collection, maintenance, and remit funds to your NRO account. Typical yields in Mangalore: 2.5–4% annually.

How do taxes work on sale in the future?

Long-term capital gains (after 2 years) attract 20% tax with indexation. Section 54 exemption available if reinvested in another property. We connect you with CAs specialising in NRI taxation.

Can I repatriate the money if I sell later?

Yes — up to USD 1 million per financial year, post taxes, is repatriable under FEMA. Sale proceeds of up to 2 residential properties per lifetime are fully repatriable.
