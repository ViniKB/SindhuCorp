Our Journey

From one address to a skyline you can trust.

Sindhu Corporation started its journey with a single apartment and a simple idea — build well, and people will come back. Two decades later, families across Mangalore live in homes we built, recommend us to relatives, and return to us for their next chapter.

Our projects — including Sindhu Nethravathi in Katipalla and the flagship Sindhu Enclave in Gandhinagar — are known across the city for their construction quality, honest pricing and long post-possession support.
