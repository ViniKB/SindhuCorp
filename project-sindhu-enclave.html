Overview

Built for families who want space, views and a setting that feels curated.

Sindhu Enclave is a single-tower, G+9 residential address in Matadakani 4th Cross, Gandhinagar — one of Mangalore's most sought-after neighbourhoods for families seeking proximity to schools, hospitals, the city core and the coastline.

The project spans a 0.69-acre parcel with 63 apartments. Basement and ground floors host ample covered parking. Every residential floor offers three 2 BHK and four 3 BHK apartments — all vaastu-compliant, cross-ventilated and thoughtfully laid out for everyday living. Select apartments on higher floors enjoy sea and river views.

Amenities are engineered for a complete lifestyle — a gym, landscaped garden, children's play area, fire and CCTV safety systems, intercom, lifts, 24×7 water and power backup, solar lighting and a sewage-treatment plant.
