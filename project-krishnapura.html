Overview

A quiet, family-first address that has aged well.

Sindhu Krishnapura was built to serve families looking for a calmer pocket of Mangalore — close to the highway for connectivity, but set back enough that you don't feel the traffic. A decade on, the landscaping has matured, the residents have grown into a real community, and the building still looks and feels as solid as the day we delivered it.

Apartments here are practical, well-proportioned 2 BHKs with good cross-ventilation and dependable build quality. The gated complex has a functional amenity set — covered parking, power backup, security, water supply and landscaped common areas.

We regularly help new families find resale or rental units here. If you're interested, let us know your requirements and we'll connect you.
