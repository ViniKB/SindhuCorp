Frequently asked

Quick answers before we speak

Are your projects RERA-registered?

Yes. Every Sindhu Corporation project is registered with Karnataka RERA. Sindhu Nethravathi, for example, is registered under PRM/KA/RERA/1257/334/PR/200811/003539.

Can I visit a completed project before deciding?

Absolutely — we encourage it. We'll walk you through a completed Sindhu address, introduce you to residents if they're available, and answer every question honestly.

Do you help with home loans?

Yes. We partner with multiple leading banks and NBFCs to help you get pre-approved quickly and at competitive rates. No hidden referral charges.

Is there post-possession support?

Yes. Every Sindhu home comes with a defect-liability period and a customer-service team that continues to serve you long after handover.

Are apartments vaastu-compliant?

Yes — vaastu alignment, cross-ventilation and natural light are non-negotiable in every floor plan we design.

Can NRIs buy a Sindhu apartment?

Yes. Our sales team handles NRI documentation, remote site visits over video and the full e-registration process. Share your details and we'll guide you end to end.
