Our Story

It started with a single apartment — and a stubborn standard.

Sindhu Corporation began with one building, a small team, and a simple promise: we will not cut corners, even when no one is watching. That promise became our first project. The first project brought the second. And the second brought the families who would go on to recommend us to their relatives, their colleagues, and their friends.

Today, we've delivered projects across Mangalore — in Katipalla, Gandhinagar, Krishnapura, Urwa and beyond — and we're building the next wave of addresses in Chilimbi and other neighbourhoods. The standard hasn't changed.

Every project we touch is RERA-registered. Every layout is vaastu-compliant. Every handover is on time and on spec. And when you move in, our relationship doesn't end — it begins.

~2003
What we stand for

Four values. Non-negotiable.

Integrity

What we promise on the brochure is what you get at handover. Written commitments, transparent pricing, honest conversations — always.

Quality

Graded materials, certified contractors, independent quality checks at every stage. We build homes to outlast the brochure.

Timeliness

We respect your calendar. Project timelines are tracked, reported honestly and met. If something shifts, you hear it first, with a clear reason.

Service

One dedicated relationship manager from enquiry to registration. A responsive service team after handover. Answers, not run-arounds.

Value

Well-located, well-built, well-priced. Every Sindhu address is chosen for long-term capital appreciation and real daily liveability.

Community

We don't just sell apartments — we build neighbourhoods. Every project is designed for safety, social life and the rhythm of families.

Leadership promise

"We don't sell square feet. We sell a home you'll live in for 30 years."
