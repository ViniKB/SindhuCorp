Overview

Built for families who want a modern home in a neighbourhood that already feels like home.

Chilimbi is one of Mangalore's most established residential pockets — close to schools, hospitals, shopping, markets and places of worship, with a quiet rhythm that families have preferred for generations. Our upcoming Sindhu Chilimbi project brings contemporary residential design to this trusted address.

We are planning thoughtfully sized 2 & 3 BHK residences, a full amenity stack including rooftop features, covered parking, and the same engineering discipline and transparency Sindhu has been known for across Mangalore.

Early access is being offered to customers on our priority list and through our upcoming app. Register your interest below — you'll be among the first to receive pricing, floor plans and launch invitations.
