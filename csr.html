We believe a city is more than the sum of its buildings. Mangalore has given us everything — here's some of what we give back, quietly and consistently, every year.

Our commitment

Four pillars of community work

We don't chase CSR for PR. We focus on a handful of long-term initiatives where we can measurably help — and stay committed to them year after year.

🎓 Education access

We sponsor the education of 48 children from Dakshina Kannada district every year — full tuition, books, uniforms, transport. Partnership with two local Kannada-medium schools and Vidyaposhak Foundation.

🌊 Coastal & environment

Quarterly beach clean-up drives across Panambur, Tannirbhavi and Someshwar beaches. Mangrove restoration along Netravati and Gurupura estuaries. 12,000+ trees planted across project sites since 2015.

👷 Worker welfare

On-site housing, clean sanitation, medical camps for construction workers and their families. Free schooling for workers' children via our "Nirmata" programme. Zero tolerance for child labour — audited quarterly.

🏥 Healthcare support

Annual contributions to Father Muller Medical College for underprivileged patient care. Free medical camps in coastal villages during monsoon. Blood donation drives every 6 months.

Sustainability

The way we build matters

Construction is one of the most resource-intensive industries on earth. Here's how we try to reduce our footprint on every project.

🌧️ Rainwater harvesting

Every project since 2010 has included mandatory rainwater harvesting infrastructure — reducing groundwater draw by an estimated 30%.

☀️ Solar lighting

Common area lighting on solar in all projects since 2018. Individual solar water heater provisions in all flats.

♻️ Waste segregation

Mandatory dry/wet waste segregation infrastructure in every building. Composting for organic waste where possible.

🧱 Fly-ash bricks

All Sindhu projects use fly-ash based bricks instead of traditional clay bricks — reducing CO₂ by ~20% vs. conventional construction.

💧 STP on-site

Sewage treatment plants on site for every large project, treated water recycled for landscaping and flushing.

🌳 Native landscaping

Landscaping uses native Mangalore species — mango, jackfruit, coconut, palash — not imported ornamentals that need heavy water.

"A good builder builds houses. A good citizen builds the city."

We try to be both. Every day, every project, every year.
