Overview

A sensible, well-built home on the right side of Mangalore.

Sindhu Nethravathi sits on Church Road in Katipalla, directly opposite the MRPL petrol pump — a settled, well-connected pocket of Mangalore with good road access to MRPL, the port area and the city. The project was built by Sindhu Corporation and is known in the area for its construction quality and layout efficiency.

Apartments are 2 BHK, ranging from 795 to 1000 sq.ft, designed for cross-ventilation, natural light and vaastu alignment. Amenities include 24×7 water supply, 24×7 backup electricity, fire safety, a gym and a children's play area.

The project is officially RERA-registered under PRM/KA/RERA/1257/334/PR/200811/003539.
